Capital Gains Tax Rate Provisions - File Architecture

Raw Statute Text
25% Subparagraph (E) - Unrecaptured Section 1250 Gain

(E) 25 percent of the excess (if any) of—

(i) the unrecaptured section 1250 gain (or, if less, the net capital gain (determined without regard to paragraph (11))), over

(ii) the excess (if any) of—
    (I) the sum of the amount on which tax is determined under subparagraph (A) plus the net capital gain, over
    (II) taxable income

28% Subparagraph (F) - Collectibles & 1202 Gain

(F) 28 percent of the amount of taxable income in excess of the sum of the amounts on which tax is determined under the preceding subparagraphs of this paragraph.

Key Insight: Rates are already in 1.rac

The 25% and 28% rates are defined as parameters in 1.rac (lines 165-177), not in subparagraph files. The new E.rac and F.rac duplicate these rate definitions. Paragraph (h)(1) reads as one integrated computation: "the tax... shall not exceed the sum of (A) plus (B) plus... (F)".

The Problem: 1.rac Violates "Filepath = Citation"
❌

1.rac has content that belongs in child files

The RAC spec is clear: "Each file encodes the DEEPEST subsection that defines it." The statute says "(E) 25 percent..." - so the 25% rate belongs in E.rac, not 1.rac. The statute says "(F) 28 percent..." - so the 28% rate belongs in F.rac, not 1.rac.
